# NebulaAI – Pitch Script

## Opening Hook (30 seconds)

"What if your laptop could join a supercomputer right now?

AI is transforming the world — but only for those who can afford $10,000 GPU clusters.
Students can't afford them. Researchers wait weeks for compute access.

Meanwhile — **millions of devices sit idle**, right now, doing nothing.

We built NebulaAI.
It turns those idle devices into a self-organizing AI supercomputer."

---

## The Problem (30 seconds)

"The compute gap in AI is real:
- A single training run on GPT-3 costs $4.6 million
- Students pay $1–3/hour just for a basic GPU on AWS
- 70% of research institutions cite compute access as their #1 bottleneck

Meanwhile, the average laptop is idle 80% of the day."

---

## The Solution (45 seconds)

"NebulaAI is an Autonomous AI Infrastructure Intelligence System.

Here's how it works:

1. Devices join the NebulaAI network — just run our agent
2. Our AI Orchestrator takes your training request in plain English
3. Five specialized AI agents collaborate:
   - The Planner agent analyzes your intent
   - The Scheduler selects the best nodes using trust scores
   - The Verifier ensures feasibility
   - The Optimizer tunes hyperparameters
   - The Recovery agent handles failures automatically

4. Training runs across the cluster — with live dashboards

It's like Google Cloud AI — but built by the community, for the community."

---

## Live Demo Walkthrough (90 seconds)

1. **Dashboard** – Show 5 live nodes, running jobs, accuracy metrics
2. **AI Chat** – Type "Train best model fast" → watch 5 agents chain
3. **Node Map** – Show cluster topology with trust-tier coloring
4. **Training Monitor** – Watch live loss/accuracy curves update
5. **Failure Simulation** – Kill Quasar → Recovery Agent reallocates in 8s
6. **Trust Leaderboard** – Show Atlas earning Pioneer badge
7. **Digital Twin** – Show predicted vs actual runtime: 97.3% accuracy

---

## Impact (30 seconds)

"If just 10,000 students share their devices:
- 15 petaFLOPS of distributed compute — equivalent to a top-50 supercomputer
- Training costs drop from $3/hour to near $0
- Research that takes weeks happens in hours

We're not just building software.
We're democratizing AI infrastructure."

---

## Vision / Closing (30 seconds)

"Our vision: every idle device becomes a node.
Students earn compute credits by sharing.
Researchers access AI at near-zero cost.

We call this: The Community Compute Layer for AI.

NebulaAI — infrastructure intelligence, for everyone."
